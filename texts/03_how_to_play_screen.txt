                        How to play:                                 
                                                                     
KEYBOARD:                                                            
                                                                     
* use 'W' 'A' 'S' 'D' to move your character                         
* use 'E' key to interact with surroundings (e.g. disarm mines, pick 
  up items, open boxes, drop or use item form inventory)             
* '1' opens backpack                                                 
* '2' shows instructions                                             
* '3' shows controls                                                 
* '4' shows credits
* '5' exits game
